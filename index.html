Open Data Portal

Open Government Data is a characteristic of modern public administration, manifested through a set of policies to promote transparency and accountability, stimulating the direct participation of citizens in the decision.

One of the main pillars of open government is making data publicly available and contribute to the growth of the community, creating seamless data experience where citizens can successfully access data and utilize key information and insights to make informed operational decisions.

Through Open Data Portal, organizations and institutions can manage and publish data in a secured way, powering community initiatives and encouraging innovation.

Feature highlight of Open Data Portal

  • supports all types of file formats
  • dynamically enables visualization/preview for formats as PDF, Excel or rendering geolocation-based elements on a map
  • API driven platform
  • permission-based access control
  • optimized for mobile devices

Powering community initiatives

With access to data, public administration encourages innovation and digital transformation, where organizations, communities and individuals contribute to the digital ecosystem, building software applications for web and mobile.

Integrating with open data sets as public transportation, air quality, parking, both the public administration officials and citizens can take better documented decisions.
